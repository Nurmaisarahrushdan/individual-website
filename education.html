🥇 I start my first education journey from kindergarten at Tadika Kemas Kota Bharu which was from 2006 until 2007. Next, I went to Sekolah Kebangsaan Zainab (2) for primary school which it was from 2008 until 2013. I also took an important examination which is Ujian Penilaian Sekolah Rendah (UPSR) during my final year of primary school in 2013. On 2014, I entered secondary school at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Ahmad Maher until 2018. Before graduating from high school, I took Sijil Peperiksaan Malaysia (SPM) examination and furthering my study at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM) Machang, Kelantan. In addition, all my schools were located at Kota Bharu, Kelantan because it was my hometown and I use to live at Kota Bharu with my family since childhood. 📚
