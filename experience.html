I do have working experience since I am turning 18 years old back in 2019. During that time, I used to work at a small company in G-Orange Mall, Kota Bharu, Kelantan. The job that I take over was by selling the women accessories. I have learned a lot during my first job ever. I got the opportunity to experience how to serve the customers, how good or bad the quality of each product, how to manage the workplace and how to keep the input from the business.

The other year, in 2020 which was during pandemic era of covid-19. My passion in baking keeps improving since we are all stuck at home and do not have lot of things to do. Baking the chocolate chip cookies and red velvet cakes is one of my favourite recipes to try. Until one day, my family ask me to change my interest from a hobby to a business. From that, I start selling chocolate chip cookies and red velvet cakes. But it is just a small business, and my customers only comes from my surrounding.

On 2021, I got a job offer at Aeon Mall Kota Bharu, Kelantan. The job was only a parttime job which last for two months only. I work at a small booth and sell women clothes. It was a meaningful work experience for me because I could work together with my best friend during our semester break. I remember for our first salary, we could travel to Penang, Malaysia. We went there and using our own money as appreciation for our hard work.

During my diploma years from 2019 until early 2022, I got several skills that will be useful for my job opportunities in the future. The skills that I manage to learn is such as using the software like Microsoft Excel, Words, PowerPoint, or Adobe Photoshop, In Design and more. The other skills include management and IT skills especially in keeping the information.
